About Michelle

I started my coding journey in February 2021, and ever since I've discovered my love of coding. After signing up for SheCodes workshops, I realize that I wanted to turn my love for coding into a career change. Coding and tech has always been something I picked up fairly quickly, and over the last year I've been expanding my skills, furthering my education, and embarking on my coding career journey! I look forward to being able to continue creating new projects, expanding my knowledge, and growing into a new role as a web developer.
